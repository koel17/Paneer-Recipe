Shahi Paneer Recipe

A royal and creamy North Indian delicacy.

Ingredients Steps Cooking Tips

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 2 medium onions (pureed)
  • 3 medium tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tbsp cashew nuts (soaked and blended)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp ghee or butter
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat ghee or butter in a pan and saut√© the onion paste until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Stir in the turmeric, red chili powder, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the cashew nut paste and cream, cooking for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the paneer cubes and garam masala. Let it simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.

Cooking Tips

Tip 1: For a richer flavor, lightly fry the paneer cubes before adding them to the gravy.

Tip 2: Use fresh cream for the best texture and taste.
